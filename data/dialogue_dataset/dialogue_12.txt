Is the coronavirus 229E the same as covid-19?

Dialogue
Patient:
Is the coronavirus 229E the same as covid-19?
Doctor:
CoV229e is one of several coronaviruses that cause common colds. Biologically related to SARS-CoV-2 (the cause of COVID-19), but different viruses.
