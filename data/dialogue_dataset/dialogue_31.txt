Can taking extra vitamin C every day help prevent you from catching the coronavirus or the flu? If so, how much vitamin C is recommended?

Dialogue
Patient:
Can taking extra vitamin C every day help prevent you from catching the coronavirus or the flu? If so, how much vitamin C is recommended?
Doctor:
In brief: Vitamin C
While getting enough vitamin C in your diet is highly recommended, it has no special ability to prevent coronavirus or flu virus.
Best preventative measures for these are frequent handwashing and avoiding people with sneezing/coughing/cold symptoms and fevers.
